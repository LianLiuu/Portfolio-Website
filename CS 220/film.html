LABOR AS PORTRAIT

Labor as Portrait is a film created for an assignment of my first-year video production class. The theme was to portray a form of labor and the people performing it with the camera. I centered my film around custodians of Wellesley College to show the constant labor that goes unnoticed in maintaining our everyday living.

Foreign Places is a film about finding a sense of home when living in a foreign place. The juxtaposition of public and personal space is portrayed through a phone call I had with my mother, and Boston's cityscape.
